Brand direction and visual foundations

Branding begins with the impression the business needs to create. We consider the audience, service type, market position and practical ways the identity will be used before developing visual direction.

A useful identity must work on small social icons, website headers, documents and larger marketing graphics.

  • Audience and positioning review
  • Color and typography direction
  • Visual mood and references
  • Practical usage planning

Logo development and refinement

Logo work can include a new concept or refinement of an existing mark. We focus on readability, balance and adaptability rather than unnecessary detail.

Final delivery can include primary, simplified and icon variations where included in the project scope.

  • Primary logo direction
  • Icon or simplified mark
  • Light and dark usage versions
  • Export guidance for digital use

Social media and campaign graphics

We create design systems for posts, stories, covers and promotional campaigns. Templates maintain consistency while allowing different content types to feel distinct.

Graphics are prepared for the intended platform dimensions and written content.

  • Post and carousel layouts
  • Story concepts
  • Campaign banners
  • Profile and cover graphics

Business documents and marketing material

A consistent brand should extend beyond social media. Proposals, service profiles, presentations and basic promotional assets can use the same visual system.

This makes the business appear organized across every customer touchpoint.

  • Proposal and profile layouts
  • Presentation direction
  • Digital flyers and banners
  • Email and document graphics

Brand guidelines

Simple guidelines help a team use the identity consistently. Depending on scope, this can cover logo spacing, approved colors, typefaces, image style and examples of correct use.

Guidelines reduce accidental changes that make the brand look inconsistent over time.

  • Logo usage rules
  • Color references
  • Typography hierarchy
  • Example applications

Frequently asked questions

Can you design a new logo?

Yes. Logo concept and visual identity work can be included according to the required number of directions and final formats.

Can you use my current logo?

Yes. We can retain an existing logo and create a more consistent system around its colors, typography and usage.

Do you provide editable files?

Editable file delivery depends on the agreed package and software used. This is confirmed before the project begins.

Can you design for UK businesses?

Yes. Visual direction can be adapted to the industry, audience and level of formality expected in the UK market.

Does branding include social media posts?

Brand identity and ongoing social content can be separate services, or a package can include initial post templates.
