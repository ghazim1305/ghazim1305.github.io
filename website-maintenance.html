Ongoing care after a website goes live

A website is a working business asset. Contact details change, software receives updates, forms can fail and new services need to be added. A maintenance plan provides a defined route for routine improvements and technical support instead of waiting until a visible problem affects customers.

Prime Growth PK offers website maintenance for suitable business websites in Pakistan, the UK and international markets. The exact plan depends on the platform, hosting access and condition of the existing website.

Content and page updates

Routine updates can include service text, team information, images, banners, FAQs, pricing notes and new landing pages. We keep headings, internal links and metadata consistent so that small edits do not gradually damage the structure of the site.

  • Text and image updates
  • New service or campaign pages
  • Navigation and footer changes
  • Form and call-to-action updates
  • Basic on-page SEO checks

Technical checks and bug fixing

Maintenance can cover broken links, display problems, form errors, script conflicts and mobile usability issues. We first identify whether the problem belongs to the website code, hosting, domain, email service or a third-party plugin, because each has a different solution and level of control.

Major redevelopment, malware recovery or unsupported legacy systems may require a separate quotation rather than routine maintenance hours.

Performance and mobile experience

Pages can become slower as large images, tracking scripts and new features are added. We review obvious performance issues, optimise suitable assets and check important pages on common mobile sizes. Performance work is prioritised around real bottlenecks rather than chasing a perfect laboratory score that may not reflect the user experience.

Security, backups and software updates

For supported platforms, maintenance may include software or dependency updates, backup checks and review of obvious security risks. No website can be described as completely secure, but timely updates, controlled access and reliable backups reduce avoidable risk.

Hosting-level security, email security and third-party account protection remain shared responsibilities and may require coordination with the relevant provider.

Forms, analytics and conversion checks

A website can appear normal while enquiries are not being delivered. We can test important forms, thank-you pages, telephone links and WhatsApp actions. Analytics and conversion tags can also be reviewed after layout or form changes.

This is especially useful for websites supporting Google Ads, SEO or social campaigns, where a broken enquiry route can waste traffic and budget.

SEO maintenance and search visibility support

Maintenance is not a replacement for a full SEO campaign, but it can protect the technical and content foundations. We can monitor indexing issues, update metadata when pages change, maintain internal links and submit revised sitemaps after significant additions.

Separate SEO support is recommended when the goal includes keyword research, content expansion, local visibility, authority building and regular performance reporting.

Flexible monthly or task-based support

Some organisations need a predictable monthly allowance, while others need support for a specific list of improvements. We agree the scope, response expectations and unused-hour policy before work begins.

Urgent out-of-hours support is not assumed unless it is explicitly included in the agreement. Clear boundaries help both sides plan realistically.

Frequently asked questions

Can you maintain a website that another developer built?

Often, yes. We first review the platform, code quality, hosting access and current condition before confirming what can be supported.

Does maintenance include unlimited changes?

No. Plans normally include an agreed allowance or task scope. Larger redesigns, new systems and major recovery work are quoted separately.

Can you fix website forms that are not sending emails?

Yes, subject to access. The issue may involve the form code, hosting, DNS or email provider, so diagnosis is completed before the solution is confirmed.

Does website maintenance improve SEO?

It can protect technical quality and keep content accurate, but competitive SEO normally requires separate research, content and authority work.

Can you provide emergency support?

Urgent support may be available by agreement, but it is not automatically included in a standard maintenance plan.
